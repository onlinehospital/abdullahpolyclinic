🦴 FRAX® Fracture Risk Tool

Predicts your 10-year probability of major osteoporotic fracture and hip fracture
combining clinical risk factors with Bone Mineral Density (BMD) data.

👤 Patient Information

📊 Bone Mineral Density (BMD) — Optional but Improves Accuracy

💡 What is T-Score? A T-score compares your bone density to a healthy 30-year-old. Normal: above -1.0 | Osteopenia: -1.0 to -2.5 | Osteoporosis: below -2.5. Get a DXA scan from your doctor to measure this accurately.
